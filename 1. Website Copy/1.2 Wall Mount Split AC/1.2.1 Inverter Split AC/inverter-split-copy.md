---

**Feature 1 — Text LEFT / Image RIGHT**

**Inverter Compressor — Rapid Response, Continuous Control**

Fixed-speed compressors run at full load or cut out completely. The inverter compressor adjusts output continuously — reaching 18°C outlet air in 30 seconds in cooling mode and 40°C outlet air in 60 seconds in heating mode. The fuzzy control algorithm delivers 10%–20% more energy saving than ordinary inverter ACs by matching compressor frequency precisely to demand. ---

**Feature 2 — Image LEFT / Text RIGHT**

**R32 Refrigerant — Fewer Refills, Full EU Compliance**

R32 carries a Global Warming Potential of 675 — well within F-gas compliance thresholds for EU and Western European markets, where high-GWP refrigerants face import and service restrictions. Every iCLIMA AIR inverter split AC ships with R32 pre-charged from the factory — no retrofitting, no refrigerant substitutions, no customs delays on F-gas grounds. R32 requires lower refrigerant volume per installation than older-generation refrigerants, reducing your buyers' maintenance cost per unit and simplifying field servicing.

---

**Feature 3 — Text LEFT / Image RIGHT**

**High Ambient Performance — Rated to 60°C, Heating to -20°C**

Most AC units derate below rated capacity once ambient temperatures pass 43°C. iCLIMA AIR inverter split AC runs at full rated capacity at 50°C ambient and continues cooling at 60°C — covering the peak summer conditions in the Middle East, Sub-Saharan Africa, and South Asia without performance loss. The heat pump variant heats down to -20°C ambient, covering cold-climate European and Central Asian markets from the same product range.

**Feature 4 — Image LEFT / Text RIGHT**

**SEER A++ / A+++ and Gen Mode — One Unit, Multiple Markets**

EU tender specifications require SEER A++ minimum — iCLIMA AIR units carry both A++ and A+++ ratings. For markets with unreliable grid supply, Gen Mode runs the unit at 30%, 50%, or 70% rated power — letting the unit operate normally from a generator without tripping. Both capabilities ship from the same factory. Your distributor in Germany and your importer in West Africa order the same unit, configured for their market.
