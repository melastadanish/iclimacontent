## SECTION 4 — PRODUCT-TO-APPLICATION MATCHING

### Matching the Right Unit to the Application

Specifying the wrong unit type costs the project money at installation and every year after. iCLIMA AIR uses four questions to match product to application: space geometry, noise threshold, access for installation, and energy source.

**Bedrooms and hotel rooms — Inverter Split AC**
The wall-mounted inverter split system produces 18°C outlet air within 30 seconds of startup. 1W standby power means the thermostat holds set-point overnight without audible compressor cycling. SEER A++/A+++ reduces the electricity cost per occupied room-night.

**Lobbies, reception areas, and retail floors — 4-Way Cassette AC**
A single cassette unit distributes airflow in 360° from a ceiling-mounted position, eliminating cold spots across high-traffic, open-plan areas. The DC motor holds noise level well below the 42 dB(A) threshold that triggers occupant complaints.

**Open halls, warehouses, and sports facilities — Floor Standing AC**
The floor standing unit produces 2,300 m³/h airflow with a 15m horizontal throw. No ceiling mounting required; the unit stands against a wall and connects to standard power. Ideal where ceiling height or load-bearing capacity rules out suspended units.

**Offices and classrooms with existing ductwork — Ducted AC**
The ducted air handler sits concealed above a false ceiling, connecting to existing supply and return ducts. A single outdoor condenser serves multiple rooms, reducing the number of outdoor units on the facade.

**Off-grid schools, clinics, and telecoms sites — Solar AC**
DC input range 80–380V accepts direct PV panel output via MC4 plug-and-play connectors. Three operating modes — pure PV, PV+grid, and grid only — allow the system to draw from panels during daylight and switch automatically at night. T3-rated: full cooling capacity at 54°C ambient, maximum operation to 68°C. AI MPPT efficiency exceeds 99%.

## SECTION 9 — FAQ

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I size the right AC unit for a commercial application?**
A: BTU requirement depends on floor area, ceiling height, occupancy load, solar gain through glazing, and the local design temperature. As a baseline, 12,000 BTU covers approximately 15–20 sqm in a standard-insulation commercial space. iCLIMA AIR's sales team will confirm the right BTU range and unit type for your application within 24 hours of receiving the project brief.

**Q: What energy efficiency ratings do iCLIMA AIR units carry?**
A: The inverter split AC achieves SEER A++/A+++ under EU standards and 25 SEER2 under North American standards. The Free Match multi-split system carries SEER ≥6.1 and SCOP ≥4.0 (EU) with an A++/A+ energy label. These ratings are confirmed on the official certification documentation supplied with every unit.

**Q: What are the installation requirements for commercial projects?**
A: Wall-mounted split systems require a penetration for refrigerant lines and drainage. Cassette units require a ceiling void for mounting. Ducted units connect to existing ductwork. The window AC installs in three steps into a standard window aperture. iCLIMA AIR provides full installation manuals and, on request, technical specifications for M&E coordination drawings.

**Q: What warranty is provided on iCLIMA AIR units?**
A: Warranty terms are confirmed at the time of order. iCLIMA AIR's <0.1% defect rate and 100% vacuum leak detection on every unit reflect the factory's position on quality control. Contact the sales team to confirm warranty structure for your market and volume.

**Q: How does iCLIMA AIR compare with other AC manufacturers sourced from China?**
