**Feature 1 — Text LEFT / Image RIGHT**

**3-Step Installation. No Outdoor Unit.**

Window AC installs without an outdoor condenser unit — the compressor, condenser, and evaporator are housed in a single chassis that sits in the window frame. iCLIMA AIR window AC installs in three steps: place the unit in the window opening, secure the side bracket, and secure the side panels. No refrigerant line set. No outdoor unit wall-mount. No vacuum pump required on site. For distributors targeting markets where installation labour cost is a key purchasing decision, 3-step install reduces the barrier to sale.

**Feature 2 — Image LEFT / Text RIGHT**

**R290 Refrigerant · CE/CB/UL Certified**

R290 (propane) refrigerant has a global warming potential of 3 — among the lowest of any AC refrigerant in production use. iCLIMA AIR window AC uses R290 as standard, ahead of regulatory moves in Europe and North America toward low-GWP refrigerants. CE, CB, and UL certifications are confirmed — covering European, global baseline, and North American import clearance without re-testing at destination. Optional WiFi connectivity is available for markets where smart control is a specification requirement.

**Feature 3 — Text LEFT / Image RIGHT**

**42 dB(A) Cooling · 32 dB(A) Fan Mode · Long-Throw Air Supply**

At 42 dB(A) in cooling mode and 32 dB(A) in fan-only mode, this window unit operates at noise levels suitable for residential bedrooms and single-occupant rooms. The optimised duct design delivers long-distance air supply — moving cooled air further into the room than a standard window unit discharge pattern. Top discharge keeps the airflow above occupant level, reducing direct cold air exposure at seated or sleeping height.

**Feature 4 — Image LEFT / Text RIGHT**

**Self-Cleaning Reminder · WiFi Option · Top Discharge Design**

Built-in filter maintenance reminder prompts the user when the filter needs cleaning — reducing the accumulation of dust that degrades airflow and cooling performance over time. Optional WiFi connectivity allows app-based control for distributors whose customers expect smart home integration. Top discharge design differentiates the unit aesthetically from traditional front-discharge window AC and improves room air circulation pattern.

---

**Feature 5 — Text LEFT / Image RIGHT**

**Single-Room Coverage · Residential Retrofit · No Wall Penetration**

Window AC is the default solution for residential retrofit in buildings where wall penetration for refrigerant line sets is not permitted — apartment buildings with restrictions on external modification, heritage buildings, rental properties. Unlike a split system that requires an outdoor condenser unit and an indoor air handler mounted separately, window AC consolidates the entire cooling capacity into one chassis. For distributors targeting the North American, West African, and Caribbean single-room residential market, window AC provides air conditioning coverage without the structural modification that split AC requires. 9,000–24,000 BTU covers studio to large single-room applications.

**Q: What BTU window AC does iCLIMA AIR manufacture?**
iCLIMA AIR window AC is available from 9,000 BTU through 24,000 BTU — covering studio rooms through large single-room residential applications. All units use R290 refrigerant as standard, with a global warming potential of 3, making the range suitable for importers entering markets where low-GWP refrigerant compliance is becoming a specification requirement. **Q: How does window AC installation work?**
iCLIMA AIR window AC installs in three steps: place the unit in the window opening, secure the side bracket, and secure the side panels. No refrigerant line set is required. No outdoor condenser unit wall-mount. No vacuum pump on site. The entire cooling capacity — compressor, condenser, and evaporator — is housed within a single chassis that sits in the window frame, making this the lowest-labour-cost AC installation format available for single-room residential applications.

**Q: What warranty does iCLIMA AIR provide on window AC?**
Every iCLIMA AIR window AC ships with a manufacturer warranty — terms are confirmed at order stage and documented in the sales agreement. Contact us before committing to volume to receive warranty documentation for your target market.

**Q: What is R290 refrigerant and why does it matter for window AC?**
R290 (propane) has a global warming potential of 3 — among the lowest of any refrigerant in commercial AC production use, compared to previous-generation refrigerants that carry a GWP measured in the hundreds or thousands.
