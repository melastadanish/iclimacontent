## SECTION 3 — PRODUCT FEATURES (Alternating Left / Right)

**Heading:** Five Commercial Unit Types. One Manufacturer. One Lead Time.

---

**Feature 1 — Text LEFT / Image RIGHT**

**4-Way Cassette — 360° Airflow · Commercial Coverage**

The 4-way cassette installs flush into a suspended ceiling and discharges airflow in four directions simultaneously — 360° coverage across the floor area below. This eliminates the hot spots and cold zones that single-direction units create in open commercial spaces. The round flow cassette variant provides even wider circular discharge for spaces where the ceiling grid does not align with a standard rectangular cassette. BTU range 12,000–60,000 covers single-zone commercial rooms up to large anchor store floors.

*Image placeholder: feature-1-commercial-ac.jpg*

---

**Feature 2 — Image LEFT / Text RIGHT**

**Ducted AC — Concealed Install · Three Static Pressure Options**

Ducted units install above a suspended ceiling and connect to supply and return air ductwork — no visible indoor unit in the occupied space. iCLIMA AIR offers three ducted variants: Mini Duct for low-clearance ceiling spaces, Low Static Pressure for standard commercial ceiling voids, and High Static Pressure for longer duct runs and more complex distribution layouts. For hotel corridors, hospital wards, clean rooms, and premium retail environments where design does not permit visible indoor units, the ducted range provides full commercial cooling without any equipment visible in the room.

*Image placeholder: feature-2-commercial-ac.jpg*

---

**Feature 3 — Text LEFT / Image RIGHT**

**Ceiling & Floor — Dual Mount · Compact Profile**

The ceiling & floor unit mounts either horizontally against the ceiling or vertically at floor level — the same unit, two installation positions. This flexibility serves commercial spaces where ceiling installation is not viable: basement areas, spaces with exposed structural ceilings, or retrofit projects where a floor-mounted unit is the only practical option. Compact and streamlined profile minimises the visual footprint in both mount positions. BTU range covers individual commercial rooms and open spaces up to 60,000 BTU.

*Image placeholder: feature-3-commercial-ac.jpg*

---

**Feature 4 — Image LEFT / Text RIGHT**

**Floor Standing — 2,300 m³/h · 15m Throw · Large Open Spaces**

The floor standing unit stands independently on the floor and discharges air horizontally across the room — 2,300 m³/h airflow, 15m throw distance. It covers large open areas — warehouse floors, mosque prayer halls, sports halls, large retail units — without ceiling installation infrastructure. DC motor operation delivers the airflow output with inverter compressor temperature control. 3D airflow (vertical and horizontal auto-swing) distributes cooled air throughout the full space volume. Wind Avoid Me feature diverts airflow direction away from the nearest occupant at a single tap.

## SECTION 11 — FAQ

**Heading:** Frequently Asked Questions — Commercial AC

**Q: What BTU range does iCLIMA AIR commercial AC cover?**
iCLIMA AIR commercial AC covers 12,000 to 60,000 BTU across all five unit types — 4-way cassette, ducted (mini, low static, and high static), ceiling & floor, console, and floor standing. A 12,000 BTU cassette unit suits a single commercial meeting room or hotel room; a 60,000 BTU floor standing unit covers large open areas such as warehouse floors and mosque prayer halls. Contact iCLIMA AIR with your floor area and ambient temperature to receive a sizing recommendation.

**Q: What is the difference between cassette, ducted, and floor standing commercial AC?**
The 4-way cassette installs into a suspended ceiling tile and discharges air in 360° — suited to open-plan offices, retail floors, and hotel lobbies with a regular ceiling grid. The ducted unit conceals entirely above a suspended ceiling and distributes air through ductwork — suited to hotel corridors, hospital wards, and premium retail where no indoor unit should be visible. The floor standing unit requires no ceiling installation at all: it stands on the floor and delivers 2,300 m³/h airflow with a 15m throw distance, covering large spaces — warehouses, sports halls, mosques — where ceiling infrastructure is not available.

**Q: What certifications does iCLIMA AIR commercial AC carry?**
iCLIMA AIR commercial AC is certified CE for Europe, CB as global baseline, UL and AHRI for North America, GMRK for the Gulf and Middle East, and ISO 9001 for quality management. Certification documentation ships with every container. For Latin American markets, RETIQ (Colombia) and RTCA (Honduras, El Salvador, Central America) certifications are also available — confirm requirements at inquiry stage and documentation will be prepared for your destination market.

**Q: What warranty does iCLIMA AIR provide on commercial AC?**
Every iCLIMA AIR commercial AC unit ships with a manufacturer warranty. Warranty terms are confirmed at order stage and documented in the sales agreement. Contact iCLIMA AIR before committing to volume to receive warranty documentation specific to your unit type and target market.

**Q: How do I choose the right commercial AC type for my project?**
Unit type selection depends on ceiling construction, space dimensions, design requirements, and installation access. Suspended ceiling with regular grid: 4-way cassette. Suspended ceiling with void space and duct access: ducted (mini, low, or high static depending on duct run length). No suspended ceiling, floor-level installation only: ceiling & floor unit or floor standing. Large open area without ceiling infrastructure: floor standing for maximum airflow coverage at 2,300 m³/h and 15m throw. Contact iCLIMA AIR with your project drawings for a specification recommendation — response within 24 hours.

**Q: How does sourcing commercial AC from iCLIMA AIR compare to buying from a local distributor for a project?**
A local distributor sources commercial AC from a manufacturer in China and applies a margin before the product reaches you. iCLIMA AIR is the factory — 30,000 sqm, 3,000,000 units per year, 15+ years of production, defect rate below 0.1%. Factory-direct pricing removes the distributor margin. You receive consistent specification across every unit in a project, direct control over certification and lead time, and OEM private label is available on the same 30-day production schedule if you require your own brand.
