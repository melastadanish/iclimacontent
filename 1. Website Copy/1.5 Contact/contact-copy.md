## SECTION 8 — FAQ

**Q: How quickly does iCLIMA AIR respond to an inquiry?**
A: Every inquiry receives a response within 24 hours. Inquiries submitted during GMT+8 business hours (Monday–Friday, 09:00–18:00) typically receive a same-day reply. WhatsApp inquiries follow the same response commitment.

**Q: What does the quotation include?**
A: The quotation covers unit price by model and BTU range, applicable certifications for your destination market, confirmed lead time (stock or production), and a freight indication to your nominated port. On OEM inquiries, the quotation also covers packaging customisation costs. The quotation is provided in USD unless otherwise requested.

**Q: How long does a sample take to arrive?**
A: A physical sample ships from the factory within 7 days of inquiry confirmation. Transit time to your location depends on the shipping method and destination. Express courier is available for urgent sample requests. The sample is a production-representative unit — the same specification that ships on the production order.

**Q: Which certifications are required for my market?**
A: CE for Europe; UL and AHRI for North America; RETIQ for Colombia; RTCA for Central America; GMRK for the Gulf and Middle East; CB for global acceptance. ISO 9001 covers the quality management system. Confirm your destination market in the inquiry form and the sales team will identify which certifications apply and confirm they are held on the model you are sourcing.

**Q: What is the minimum order quantity?**
A: iCLIMA AIR operates with a low MOQ. There is no requirement to commit to a full container on a first order. MOQ is confirmed per product line and BTU range in the quotation. Buyers are encouraged to start with a sample, then place an initial commercial order at a volume that matches their programme.

**Q: How is the OBM inquiry different from an OEM inquiry?**
A: An OBM inquiry specifies the iCLIMA AIR brand — the product, certifications, and design are already finalised. The sales team confirms stock availability, unit price, and lead time. An OEM inquiry requires additional information: your brand name, packaging design files (or a request to use the factory's packaging design service), and your destination market for certification re-filing. Both inquiries start with the same form and receive a response within 24 hours.
