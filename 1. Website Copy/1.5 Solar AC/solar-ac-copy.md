**Heading:** Frequently Asked Questions — Solar Air Conditioner

**Q: What BTU solar AC does iCLIMA AIR manufacture?**
iCLIMA AIR manufactures solar AC from 9,000 to 24,000 BTU. Cooling capacity selection depends on room area, insulation quality, and local ambient temperature — for T3 markets in Africa, the Middle East, and Latin America, size up by 20–25% versus a moderate-climate guide. The iCLIMA AIR solar AC is T3 rated, delivering full cooling capacity at 54°C ambient and continuing to operate at 68°C ambient. Request a sizing consultation when submitting your inquiry.

**Q: How does the solar AC work without full sun?**
iCLIMA AIR solar AC operates in three modes and switches between them automatically. When solar generation is sufficient, the unit runs in pure PV mode at no grid cost. On cloudy days or at partial irradiance, the AI switching engine moves to PV+Grid hybrid mode, supplementing solar with mains power to maintain full cooling output. At night or when solar input is unavailable, the unit draws from the grid only. The AI MPPT controller captures >99% of available panel output throughout the day — the system self-manages without any user input or power interruption.

**Q: What certifications does the iCLIMA AIR solar AC carry?**
iCLIMA AIR solar AC carries CE, CB, UL, and AHRI certifications. CE covers European market entry. CB is the global baseline accepted across most markets. UL and AHRI cover North American requirements. Additional certifications — including RETIQ (Colombia), RTCA (Central America), and GMRK (Gulf / Middle East) — are available; confirm your specific destination market requirements at the inquiry stage. No re-testing is required at customs for the markets these certifications cover.

**Q: What warranty does iCLIMA AIR provide on solar AC?**
Every iCLIMA AIR solar AC ships with a manufacturer warranty — terms are confirmed at order stage and documented in the sales agreement. Contact us to receive warranty documentation for your target market before committing to volume.

**Q: How much solar panel capacity do I need to run the solar AC?**
