### SECTION 3 — THE CHALLENGE

**Heading:** Three AC Problems That Cost Hotel Projects Money

**Guest Noise Complaints — AC That Disrupts Sleep**
A fixed-speed compressor cycles on and off at full load — the start-up surge and abrupt cutoff disturb guests during light sleep. The inverter compressor in iCLIMA AIR split AC adjusts output continuously. The thermostat maintains the set temperature without the on/off cycle. No surge noise. No cold snap from an overshooting compressor. Fewer noise complaints on review platforms means fewer bookings affected.

**Energy Bills From 24/7 Runtime**
Hotel AC runs every hour the property is occupied. SEER A+++ rated inverter units deliver 10%–20% more energy saving than ordinary inverter AC — and significantly more versus fixed-speed units running the same hours. iCLIMA AIR inverter split AC carries both SEER A++ and A+++ ratings — the highest energy class under the EU label. At 200 rooms running 12 hours per day, the rating difference reaches operating margin.

**Project Supply Failure Mid-Build**
A hotel project needs consistent specification across every room — same unit dimensions, same air handler, same certifications, same documentation — delivered on a fixed schedule. iCLIMA AIR produces 3,000,000 units per year from a single 30,000 sqm factory. Your project volume is built into scheduled production, not treated as a priority run when a slot opens.
