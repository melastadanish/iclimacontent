### SECTION 3 — THE CHALLENGE

**Heading:** Three AC Problems That Cost Office Projects Money

**Noise in Open-Plan Offices — AC That Disrupts Concentration**
Open-plan floors run air conditioning 9 or more hours every working day. A compressor cycling on and off at full load creates a start-up surge and an abrupt cutoff — ambient noise spikes that disrupt concentration and reduce meeting quality. The inverter compressor in iCLIMA AIR cassette and split units — sourced from GMCC, Highly, Rechi, or Sanyo — modulates output continuously. No on/off cycling. No spike noise. The thermostat holds the set temperature without the compressor hunting. 4-way cassette units distribute airflow evenly across 360° so no zone runs hotter and no compressor surges to compensate.

**Energy Cost in All-Day Commercial Operation**
A commercial building running air conditioning 10 hours per day across 250 working days has an energy spend that directly hits operating margin. At that runtime, the efficiency gap between an ordinary inverter unit and an A+++ rated unit compounds every month of the lease. iCLIMA AIR inverter units deliver 10%–20% more energy saving than ordinary inverter ACs — the inverter AI algorithm adjusts compressor frequency to match real-time cooling capacity demand rather than running at a fixed output. SEER A+++ rating means European commercial projects satisfy the tightest energy directives from the day the building opens.

**Project Supply Failure for Multi-Floor Fit-Outs**
A 20-floor office fit-out requires consistent specification across every floor — same unit dimensions, same air handler type, same certifications, same documentation — delivered on a programme that matches the construction schedule. If the supplier runs short on a SKU mid-project, the programme slips and penalty clauses apply. iCLIMA AIR produces 3,000,000 units per year from a single 30,000 sqm factory. Project volumes are built into scheduled production. Your order is not treated as a spot purchase against available stock — it is a production run with a confirmed 30-day lead time from payment confirmation.
