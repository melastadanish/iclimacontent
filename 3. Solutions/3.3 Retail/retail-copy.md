### SECTION 4 — PRODUCTS FOR RETAIL PROJECTS

**Heading:** iCLIMA AIR Products Specified for Retail & Shopping Center Projects

---

**4-Way Cassette AC — 12,000–60,000 BTU**
Ceiling-mounted air handler with 360° airflow — the standard specification for anchor stores, department stores, and large retail floor areas. The evaporator distributes conditioned air across the full floor radius with no dead zones at corners or behind displays. Inverter compressor runs continuously to hold the thermostat set point, avoiding the warm-up lag of fixed-speed units after trading hours begin. Concealed installation — the round panel is the only visible component from the retail floor. Available in standard and compact series.
→ [[commercial-ac]]

---

**Ducted AC — Mini, Low Static, High Static**
Concealed installation above the ceiling — no visible indoor unit, no equipment exposed in the retail environment. Air distributes through ceiling grilles matched to the fit-out design. Mini duct installs inside ceiling beams where floor-to-ceiling height is constrained in older mall structures. High static pressure duct covers large conditioned spaces including food court back-of-house and loading corridors. Standard specification for premium retail where interior design standards prohibit visible AC equipment.
→ [[commercial-ac]]

---

**Ceiling & Floor AC — 12,000–60,000 BTU**
Dual installation flexibility — mounts to the ceiling or stands on the floor depending on the retail unit layout. Compact and streamlined profile suits boutiques and smaller retail units where the installation position varies by tenancy. Cooling capacity from 12,000 BTU covers a single boutique unit; larger configurations handle multi-bay retail spaces. Available in inverter variants with R32 refrigerant pre-charged at the factory.
→ [[commercial-ac]]

---

**Floor Standing AC — 2,300 m³/h Airflow**
Freestanding unit delivering 2,300 m³/h airflow with a 15-metre throw distance — designed for large open retail areas, hypermarkets, and anchor stores where ceiling mounting is not feasible. DC motor with 3D airflow (vertical and horizontal auto-swing) covers wide floor areas from a single installation point. Touch control with embedded buttons and hidden VLED display — the front panel integrates into a retail environment without industrial appearance. Wind Avoid Me function diverts airflow direction automatically at one tap — prevents cold air blowing directly on checkout staff during extended hours.
