### SECTION 3 — THE CHALLENGE

**Heading:** Three AC Problems Healthcare Projects Cannot Afford

**Temperature Consistency in Clinical Environments**
Patient wards, operating theatres, and pharmaceutical storage require temperature held within a narrow band — a thermostat that acts as a trigger switch, cycling the compressor on and off, creates swings that affect patient comfort and drug integrity. The inverter compressor in iCLIMA AIR units modulates output continuously. Once set point is reached, the compressor does not shut off — it runs at reduced frequency to hold temperature precisely. Outlet temperature remains stable without the cold overshoot and warm recovery cycle that standard fixed-speed units produce.

**Air Quality and Infection Control**
Standard AC recirculates air through the evaporator coil without treatment — in clinical settings, airborne pathogen risk makes air quality a procurement specification, not an afterthought. iCLIMA AIR's optional B.I.G. Care+ module combines UVC sterilization operating at 240–280nm wavelength with a Bipolar Ionic Generator to eliminate more than 98.66% of bacteria — available on heat pump DC inverter models. The 56°C self-cleaning cycle burns residue from the evaporator coil without chemicals, maintaining the evaporator surface between deep cleans. These are not marketing features — they are specifications with a confirmed elimination rate.

**Supply Reliability for Phased Healthcare Builds**
A hospital expansion across multiple wings requires consistent AC specification across every phase — same unit dimensions, same air handler, same certifications, same documentation. A mid-project supplier change creates commissioning mismatches and procurement administration that delays handover. iCLIMA AIR produces 3,000,000 units per year from a 30,000 sqm factory in China. Phase 1 and Phase 3 of your hospital build draw from the same production specification. Thirty-day lead time from order confirmation — confirmed at inquiry stage, not estimated on delivery.
