### SECTION 3 — THE CHALLENGE

**Heading:** Three AC Problems That Cost Warehouse and Industrial Projects Money

**Worker Productivity in High-Ambient Warehouse Environments**
Warehouse floors in the Middle East, Africa, and Southern Europe regularly exceed 40°C in summer. At these temperatures, worker productivity drops measurably and health and safety requirements mandate active cooling. iCLIMA AIR floor standing units deliver 2,300 m³/h airflow with a 15m throw distance — covering large open warehouse bays without requiring ceiling installation or overhead ductwork. The DC motor and 3D airflow system distribute conditioned air evenly across the floor area using vertical and horizontal auto-swing, not just directly below the unit.

**Continuous Operation in High-Ambient Industrial Conditions**
An air conditioning unit that shuts down when ambient temperature exceeds 43°C is not viable in a Gulf or West African warehouse. iCLIMA AIR inverter compressor units maintain full rated cooling capacity at 50°C ambient and continue cooling at 60°C ambient — the temperature range that industrial buildings in these markets actually experience at peak summer. The inverter compressor maintains a stable evaporator coil temperature even when outdoor ambient fluctuates through the working day. No derating. No shutdown during peak load hours.

**Large-Space Coverage at Project Cost**
Covering a 5,000 sqm warehouse with individual split units requires a large number of installed positions and extended cabling runs. For open warehouse bays, floor standing units and high-static ducted systems provide zone coverage at lower installed-unit cost than wall-mount arrays. iCLIMA AIR's commercial range runs to 60,000 BTU per unit — reducing the total number of units needed to cover a given floor area. Low MOQ means the buyer does not have to commit the full project volume in a single order. Pricing confirmed within 24 hours of inquiry.
