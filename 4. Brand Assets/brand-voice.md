# Brand Voice — iCLIMA AIR

## Positioning
A reliable, technically competent AC manufacturer supplying B2B buyers across emerging and developed markets.

## Voice Pillars
| Pillar | Means | Does Not Mean |
|---|---|---|
| Professional | Clear, structured, expert | Cold, corporate, jargon-heavy |
| Technical | Spec-led, precise, credible | Confusing, overcomplicated |
| Trustworthy | Fact-backed, honest, consistent | Boastful, vague |
| Direct | Short sentences, clear CTAs | Blunt, unhelpful |

## Reference Sites (Style)
- zerohvacr.com — clean, B2B, product-first
- airbrisk.com — professional, spec-forward

## CTAs (always use one of these)
- Primary: **Get a Quote**
- Secondary: **Contact Us on WhatsApp**

## Language at Launch
English only. French and Spanish to be added later.
