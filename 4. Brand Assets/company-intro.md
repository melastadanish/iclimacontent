# iCLIMA AIR — Company Introduction

## Core Facts
- **Founded:** 2011
- **Industry experience:** 15+ years in AC manufacturing
- **Factory location:** China
- **Factory size:** 30,000 sqm
- **Annual production capacity:** 3 million units/year
- **Total workforce:** ~10,000 people (2,000 management + production & R&D)
- **Lead time:** 30 days standard · 10 days from stock
- **Sampling:** 7-day rapid sampling
- **Defect rate:** <0.1%
- **Pre-shipment:** 100% leakage testing on every unit
- **Design:** Italian design office (brand aesthetics)
- **MOQ:** Low MOQ

Primary: Europe · North America · Africa · Latin America · Middle East
