## Brand Voice Rules (B2B)

| Rule | iCLIMA AIR standard |
|---|---|
| **Person** | Second person — "your project", "your buyers", "your market" |
| **Tone** | Direct, technical, zero fluff. No warmth without a number behind it. |
| **Claims** | Every claim traced to verified factory data. No estimates. |
| **Jargon** | Technical language is allowed — B2B buyers know their product. Do not over-explain. |
| **Lead with** | Outcomes for the buyer's business — margin, supply reliability, project fit |
| **Paragraph length** | Max 3 sentences. B2B readers skim. |
| **Numbers** | Embedded in sentences — never floated as labels or badges |
| **Urgency** | Never manufactured. B2B buyers evaluate over weeks. Urgency language damages trust. |
| **We vs you** | "You" appears more than "we" in every section |
| **First person** | Never write "At iCLIMA, we believe..." or "We are proud to..." |

## CTA Rules (B2B Adapted)

Every CTA tells the buyer exactly what they receive when they act.

**Primary CTA (conversion):**
- "Request a project quote" — for product and solution pages
- "Get pricing for your order" — for product pages

**Secondary CTA (engagement):**
- "Contact us on WhatsApp" — always specific, never generic "contact us"
- "Download the product specification sheet" — for spec-heavy sections

**CTAs that fail:**
- "Learn more" — fails substitution test
- "Contact us" — too vague (acceptable only in the contact section)
- "Get in touch" — no value exchange
- "Explore our range" — no outcome stated

**One CTA per section. Two CTAs create decision paralysis.**

## Common Failure Patterns — B2B Version

| Pattern | Example of failure | Correct approach |
|---|---|---|
| Volume claim without proof | "We supply large projects" | "3,000,000 units per year — your project volume is covered" |
| Lead time vague | "Fast delivery" | "30 days from order confirmation to production-ready units" |
| Quality claim without component proof | "High quality components" | "GMCC, Sanyo, and Highly compressors — the same suppliers used by the world's largest AC brands" |
| Capacity claim without number | "Large factory" | "30,000 sqm factory in China" |
| Experience claim without years | "Years of experience" | "15+ years of AC manufacturing" |
| OEM claim without specifics | "We offer OEM" | "Full OEM and ODM — your brand, your spec, shipped from our factory" |
| Certification claim without names | "Fully certified" | "CE, CB, UL, AHRI, RETIQ, RTCA, and GMRK certified" |
| MOQ vague | "Flexible MOQ" | "Low MOQ — entry orders accepted without unit price penalty" |
| Generic CTA | "Contact us to learn more" | "Request a project quote for your volume and spec" |
