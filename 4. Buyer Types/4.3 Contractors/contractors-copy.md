**Which certifications are available for project tenders?**
CE and CB cover European tenders and most international specifications. UL and AHRI cover North American tenders. GMRK covers Gulf region tenders. RETIQ covers Colombia. RTCA covers Central American markets. ISO 9001 confirms quality management system compliance — relevant for procurement frameworks in multiple regions.

**What is the lead time on mixed product orders?**
Mixed orders — combining inverter split, cassette, and floor standing, for example — run on a 30-day production schedule from order confirmation. All products in the order are scheduled together for delivery on a single date. Stock items can supplement project orders at 10-day dispatch.

**How do I size BTU for commercial zones?**
BTU sizing depends on zone volume, occupancy load, glazing, and local climate. iCLIMA AIR provides technical support on zone sizing for project inquiries. General guidance: office zones typically use 12,000–24,000 BTU units; lobbies and open commercial spaces scale from 24,000 BTU upward; large halls are specified with floor standing units at 12,000–60,000 BTU.

**What warranty applies to project supply?**
Warranty terms are confirmed at order placement. All units carry iCLIMA AIR's standard warranty. Projects requiring extended warranty documentation for client handover should specify this at the inquiry stage.

**Is it better to source through iCLIMA AIR or a local distributor for a project?**
Sourcing factory-direct removes a margin layer and gives direct access to certification documentation, production scheduling, and technical specification. For projects requiring custom BTU configurations or multi-product coordination, working directly with the factory gives contractors more control over specification, timeline, and documentation.
