## SECTION 3 — THE FACTORY

### A 30,000 sqm Facility That Ships 3,000,000 Units a Year

The iCLIMA AIR manufacturing facility in China runs on automated production lines across every critical assembly stage. Each step below exists because manual processes introduce variation — and variation is where defects originate.

**Automated production processes in operation:**

| Process | What it eliminates |
|---|---|
| Automatic pipe assembly welding | Manual joint inconsistency |
| Automatic compressor assembly | Torque variation, under/over-tightening |
| Automatic welding | Operator-dependent penetration depth |
| Automatic vacuum leak detection | Undetected refrigerant leaks before dispatch |
| Automatic packing | Handling damage at final stage |
| High speed punching | Fin geometry deviation at volume |

The production system is fully visualized, digitized, and traceable. Every unit can be tracked through its production stages. The result: a 20% improvement in first-pass rate and a 73% reduction in missing part and incorrect assembly PPM over the system's operating lifetime.

The facility employs approximately 10,000 people. Capacity is 3,000,000 units per year. That scale is not cited for impressiveness — it means your order does not disrupt the production schedule.
