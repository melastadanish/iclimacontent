## SECTION 3 — CERTIFICATION DETAIL

### CE — European Conformity

**Issuing body:** European Commission (self-declaration under harmonised EU directives)
**Market coverage:** All 27 EU member states plus UK
**What it covers:** All iCLIMA AIR split AC and commercial AC models meet the mechanical, electrical, and energy safety requirements defined under the applicable EU directives including the Low Voltage Directive and Ecodesign Regulation.

**What it means for your import process:** CE marking is mandatory for any AC unit placed on the EU market. A product without CE marking does not clear EU customs. With CE already on the iCLIMA AIR product, no additional conformity assessment is required at the importer level. The Declaration of Conformity is included in the shipment documentation package.

---

### CB — IEC International System Certificate

**Issuing body:** National Certification Body (NCB) under the IECEE CB Scheme
**Market coverage:** Accepted in 57+ countries as a baseline electrical safety test report
**What it covers:** Electrical safety to IEC standards, accepted across IECEE member countries as a foundation for local market approval

**What it means for your import process:** The CB certificate reduces the cost and time of gaining local market approval in countries that participate in the IECEE scheme. In many markets, CB is accepted directly or requires only minor supplementary testing rather than a full independent safety evaluation. This is the single most cost-efficient certification for buyers operating across multiple non-EU, non-North American markets.

---

### UL — Underwriters Laboratories

**Issuing body:** UL Solutions (formerly Underwriters Laboratories), USA
**Market coverage:** United States and Canada
**What it covers:** Electrical safety, construction, and performance to ANSI/UL standards; required for sale and project specification in the US and Canadian markets

**What it means for your import process:** UL listing is required for AC equipment sold through US retail channels and specified in US commercial building projects. Many US state and local codes require UL-listed equipment for permitted HVAC installations. Without UL, the product does not qualify for the majority of US residential and commercial project specs.

---

### AHRI — Air-Conditioning, Heating, and Refrigeration Institute

**Issuing body:** AHRI (Air-Conditioning, Heating, Refrigeration Institute), USA
**Market coverage:** United States and Canada
**What it covers:** Independent third-party verification of BTU output and energy efficiency ratings (SEER, EER, HSPF) as declared by the manufacturer

**What it means for your import process:** The AHRI certificate is the performance standard required in US commercial project specifications. When an engineer writes a cooling capacity requirement into a commercial project spec, they write it in AHRI-certified BTU. A product without AHRI certification cannot be substituted into a US project spec without a project engineer's written exception. For buyers targeting US commercial and government projects, AHRI is non-negotiable.

---

### RETIQ — Colombian Energy Efficiency Label

**Issuing body:** UPME (Unidad de Planeación Minero-Energética), Colombia
**Market coverage:** Colombia
**What it covers:** Mandatory energy efficiency labelling for air conditioning equipment imported and sold in Colombia; confirms the unit meets minimum energy performance standards

**What it means for your import process:** RETIQ is required by Colombian customs for AC import clearance. Without it, AC units are not legally importable or saleable in Colombia. iCLIMA AIR products carry RETIQ, which means Colombian importers receive documentation that clears the customs stage without requiring additional local testing or re-labelling.

---

### RTCA — Central American Conformity Mark

**Issuing body:** Regional Technical Body under the Central American Free Trade framework
**Market coverage:** Honduras · El Salvador · Guatemala · Nicaragua · Costa Rica
**What it covers:** Product safety and performance conformity for the Central American regional market

**What it means for your import process:** The RTCA mark gives buyers a single certification that satisfies import requirements across five Central American countries. Rather than managing five separate national approvals, buyers in the region work from one document set. This is the only certification covering all five Central American markets simultaneously.

---

### GMRK — Gulf Standards Organisation Mark

**Issuing body:** GSO (Gulf Standards Organisation)
**Market coverage:** Saudi Arabia · UAE · Kuwait · Qatar · Bahrain · Oman
**What it covers:** Product safety, energy efficiency, and performance standards as defined by GSO technical regulations for air conditioning equipment in GCC member states

**What it means for your import process:** The GMRK mark is mandatory for AC equipment imported into GCC countries. Without it, products are stopped at Gulf ports. iCLIMA AIR's GMRK certification means buyers importing into the Saudi, UAE, Kuwaiti, Qatari, Bahraini, or Omani markets have a single mark that satisfies all six customs authorities. No additional local testing is required in covered GCC states.

---

### ISO 9001 — Quality Management System

**Issuing body:** Third-party accredited certification body (ISO 9001:2015)
**Market coverage:** Global — accepted as a procurement quality requirement in all markets
**What it covers:** The manufacturing facility's documented quality management system: process controls, traceability, non-conformance handling, corrective action procedures, and management review

**What it means for your import process:** ISO 9001 is not a product certification — it is a factory certification. It means that the quality system behind the product is independently audited and documented. For procurement teams evaluating a new supplier, ISO 9001 is the minimum evidence that a factory operates a documented process rather than an undocumented one. For buyers placing orders 30 days in advance, ISO 9001 means the production system will behave the same on day 30 as it did when the sample was approved.

## SECTION 8 — FAQ

**Which certifications are required for EU import?**
CE marking is mandatory for all AC units sold in the EU. The CB certificate provides the IEC baseline electrical safety report accepted by EU customs authorities and many individual EU member state bodies. Both are included with iCLIMA AIR shipments to EU buyers.

**Which certifications are required for North American import?**
UL listing is required for US and Canadian retail and project supply. AHRI certification is required for US commercial project specifications. Both are held by iCLIMA AIR and included in shipment documentation for North American buyers.

**Which certifications are required for Gulf import?**
The GMRK mark is mandatory for AC import into Saudi Arabia, UAE, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, and Oman. iCLIMA AIR holds GMRK certification. A single GMRK document satisfies customs requirements across all six GCC member states.

**What documents ship with every order?**
Each shipment includes: applicable certificates of conformity, test reports, Declaration of Conformity (for CE), AHRI certificate data (for US/Canadian orders), and any additional market-specific documentation confirmed at the inquiry stage. No document needs to be requested separately after the container ships.

**How does OEM certification transfer work?**
For OEM orders, the existing iCLIMA AIR certification documentation is re-applied to the buyer's brand name. The product does not require independent re-testing. The re-application process is confirmed at the order stage. Documents are prepared under the buyer's brand before the shipment is packed.

**What if my market is not covered by the 8 brand certifications?**
The manufacturing facility holds certifications covering 140+ countries. For markets outside the 8 confirmed brand certifications, the process is: inquire with your destination market confirmed, and availability is checked against the factory's broader portfolio. Documentation is provided at the order stage where available. Contact [[contact]] to confirm your market.
