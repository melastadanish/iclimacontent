## SECTION 3 — THE EUROPEAN MARKET

### The European AC Market — What Importers Need to Know

Europe's AC market is driven by two demands: summer cooling in Southern and Central Europe, and year-round heat pump adoption across the continent. The EU's energy labelling system requires SEER A++ as a minimum entry standard for inverter split AC — units below this rating face market exclusion, not just lower sales. R32 refrigerant is now the standard under the EU F-Gas Regulation's phase-down schedule; R410a-charged units face increasing import restrictions. Distributors importing from China need CE certification and CB baseline safety to clear EU customs without re-testing. iCLIMA AIR holds both before any order is placed.

The European market also rewards brand identity. iCLIMA AIR's Italian-designed OBM brand carries design credibility that resonates on European shelves. ## SECTION 4 — CERTIFICATIONS FOR EUROPE

### Certifications Required to Import AC into Europe

AC units imported into the European Union without valid CE marking face confiscation, market withdrawal orders, and re-export at the importer's cost. CE is not optional — it is the legal requirement for placing HVAC products on the EU market. iCLIMA AIR holds CE before shipment; the Declaration of Conformity and technical file ship with every container.

| Certification | Issuing Body | Coverage | Why European Importers Need It |
|---|---|---|---|
| CE | European Commission — self-declaration against EU Directives | EU member states + EEA | Legal requirement for EU market access — LVD, EMC, ErP Directives |
| CB | IECEE CB Scheme | Global baseline | Mutual recognition — supports CE testing · accepted by EU notified bodies |
| ISO 9001 | Bureau Veritas / TÜV (factory-level) | Quality management | Required by large European retailers and procurement specifications |

EU ErP energy labelling (SEER A++/A+++) is a product specification requirement, not a separate certification. iCLIMA AIR's inverter split AC meets A++ minimum; top-tier models reach A+++.

R32 refrigerant compliance under EU F-Gas Regulation Phase 2 applies at product level — iCLIMA AIR split AC uses R32 as standard.

## SECTION 10 — FAQ

**Q: What certifications are required to import AC into Europe?**
CE marking is the legal requirement for placing air conditioners on the EU market. CE covers the Low Voltage Directive, EMC Directive, and ErP (Energy-related Products) Directive for HVAC products. CB certification provides the baseline IEC safety test report accepted by EU notified bodies. ISO 9001 is required by major European retail chains and institutional procurement specifications. iCLIMA AIR holds all three — documentation ships with every container, and no re-testing is required at the EU port of entry.

**Q: What SEER rating do iCLIMA AIR European products carry?**
iCLIMA AIR inverter split AC meets EU SEER A++ as a minimum energy class. Top-tier models achieve A+++. The Free Match multi-split system carries SEER ≥6.1 and SCOP ≥4.0, with an A++/A+ EU energy label. These ratings are measured to the EU ErP Directive standard — they appear on the EU energy label that ships with every unit. EER performance under AHRI is also available for buyers supplying mixed US/EU markets.

**Q: Does iCLIMA AIR use R32 refrigerant for European products?**
Yes. All iCLIMA AIR split AC products use R32 refrigerant — GWP 675, one-third that of R410a. R32 is compliant with the EU F-Gas Regulation's phase-down schedule and is the factory standard across the inverter split and Free Match range. Window AC uses R290 (propane), GWP of 3. No product in the range uses R410a.

**Q: What is the lead time for European shipments?**
Production lead time is 30 days from order confirmation. Stock items ship in 10 days. **Q: Can I import OEM private label AC under my brand into Europe?**
Yes. OEM private label is available for the European market. The CE Declaration of Conformity and EU energy label documentation are re-issued under the buyer's brand name and model designation. The underlying product — inverter compressor, R32 refrigerant, SEER A++/A+++ rating, condenser and evaporator assembly — does not change. Confirm your EU destination country at inquiry.

**Q: How does factory-direct from iCLIMA AIR compare to buying from a European wholesaler?**
A European wholesaler adds one to three margin layers between the factory and the importer. iCLIMA AIR ships factory-direct from a 30,000 sqm facility running 3,000,000 units per year — no intermediary, no rebrand cost.
