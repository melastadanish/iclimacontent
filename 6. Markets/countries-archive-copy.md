## SECTION 8 — FAQ

**Q: Does iCLIMA AIR ship to all five markets from the same factory?**
Yes. All products ship from the same 30,000 sqm facility in China — Inverter Split AC, Solar AC, Free Match AC, Window AC, and Commercial AC. The factory produces 3,000,000 units per year. Market-specific certification documentation is prepared per order based on the destination market declared at inquiry.

**Q: What certifications does iCLIMA AIR hold for international markets?**
iCLIMA AIR holds CE (Europe), CB (global baseline), UL and AHRI (North America), RETIQ (Colombia), RTCA (Honduras, El Salvador, Central America), GMRK (Gulf/Middle East), and ISO 9001. These 8 certifications are held by the iCLIMA AIR brand — not factory-level only. Documentation ships with every container.

**Q: What refrigerant do iCLIMA AIR products use?**
Split AC products use R32 refrigerant — GWP 675, one-third that of R410a. R32 is compliant with EU F-Gas Regulation, acceptable under North American EPA guidelines, and preferred in high-ambient markets. Window AC uses R290. Neither product line uses R410a.

**Q: What is the minimum order for an international shipment?**
iCLIMA AIR operates with a low MOQ policy — no large minimum order commitment on a first shipment. Volume, packaging, and pricing are confirmed within 24 hours of inquiry. Stock items ship in 10 days; production orders in 30.

**Q: Can I order OEM private label for any market?**
Yes. OEM is available for all five markets. The certifications held by iCLIMA AIR are re-applied to the buyer's brand name and model designation. Confirm your destination market at inquiry — the quotation specifies which certifications apply and the per-unit cost for packaging customisation.

**Q: What is the lead time from China to each market?**
Production lead time is 30 days from order confirmation regardless of destination market. Stock items ship in 10 days. Ocean freight transit time varies by port: Europe 25–35 days, North America (East Coast) 28–35 days, West Africa 20–28 days, Gulf 18–25 days, Latin America 30–40 days. iCLIMA AIR provides full export documentation; the buyer arranges freight or uses their own forwarder.
