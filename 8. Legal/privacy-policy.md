## SECTION 1 — INTRODUCTION

# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 1 June 2026
**Last updated:** 10 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how iCLIMA AIR ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information provided by visitors to this website and by business contacts who submit enquiries via our contact form, email, or WhatsApp.

iCLIMA AIR operates this website to connect air conditioner distributors, importers, contractors, and project developers with our manufacturing and supply services. We do not sell consumer products directly; all business conducted through this website is between iCLIMA AIR and commercial buyers.

---

## SECTION 2 — INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### What We Collect

We collect information only when you provide it to us. We do not collect personal data passively beyond standard website analytics.

**Contact form and enquiry submissions:**
When you submit a quote request or contact form, we collect: your name · company name · email address · phone number · country · product interest · estimated volume · any message content you include.

**Email and WhatsApp communications:**
Messages you send to our business email or WhatsApp number are retained as part of our commercial correspondence records.

**Website analytics:**
We use standard web analytics (Google Analytics or equivalent) to measure page views, traffic sources, and user behaviour in aggregate. This data is anonymised and does not identify individual visitors.

**Cookies:**
This website uses functional cookies required to operate the contact form and analytical cookies to measure traffic. You may decline analytical cookies without affecting your ability to use the site.

---

## SECTION 3 — HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

### How We Use Your Information

Information you submit is used to:

- Respond to your product enquiry or quote request
- Provide pricing, certification, and lead time information relevant to your market
- Manage our commercial relationship if you place an order
- Improve our website content based on aggregate analytics

We do not use your information for: automated marketing campaigns · third-party advertising · profiling or scoring · any purpose unrelated to the B2B supply relationship you initiated.

---

## SECTION 4 — WHO WE SHARE INFORMATION WITH

### Sharing of Information

iCLIMA AIR does not sell your data. We do not share your contact information with third-party marketing companies.

We may share relevant enquiry details with our manufacturing and logistics partners where this is necessary to respond to your order — for example, sharing your destination port with our freight forwarder to prepare a shipping quote. This sharing is limited to what is operationally necessary.

We use Google Analytics for website measurement. Google's privacy policy governs data processed by Google Analytics — see [google.com/policies/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## SECTION 5 — DATA RETENTION

### How Long We Keep Your Information

Enquiry records are retained for 3 years from the date of submission, or for the duration of any commercial relationship, whichever is longer. This retention period allows us to reference prior correspondence if you return with a follow-up enquiry.

If you have not placed an order and would like your enquiry record deleted, contact us at the address below and we will remove it within 30 days.

---

## SECTION 6 — YOUR RIGHTS

### Your Rights

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Access** — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Correction** — request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion** — request deletion of your data where no legal basis for retention applies
- **Objection** — object to processing based on our legitimate interests
- **Portability** — request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address in Section 8. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are located outside the EU, you retain the right to request deletion of your enquiry record at any time.

## SECTION 7 — SECURITY

### How We Protect Your Information

iCLIMA AIR takes reasonable technical measures to protect information submitted via this website: HTTPS encryption on all pages · restricted access to enquiry records · no storage of payment card data (all payments handled via bank transfer or secure payment platforms).

No internet transmission is completely secure. If you have concerns about transmitting sensitive business information via this website, contact us by email or WhatsApp directly.
