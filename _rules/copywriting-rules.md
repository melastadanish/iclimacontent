# Copywriting Rules — iCLIMA AIR

## Brand Voice
**Professional · Technical · Trustworthy · Direct**

## Audience
B2B buyers: distributors, importers, contractors, retailers.
They care about: reliability, certifications, capacity range, market compatibility, price competitiveness.

## Tone Guidelines
- Confident but not arrogant
- Technical but accessible — explain specs in business terms
- No consumer fluff (no "stay cool this summer")
- No vague claims — back every claim with a fact or figure
- Lead with the buyer's problem, then offer the solution

## Writing Rules
1. Headlines: benefit-first, specific, no puns
2. Body copy: short paragraphs (2–3 lines max), no walls of text
3. CTAs: "Get a Quote" (primary) · "Contact on WhatsApp" (secondary)
4. Numbers sell: use BTU ranges, production capacity, years in industry
5. Certifications matter: mention R32 refrigerant, SEER/SCOP ratings, energy class where relevant
6. Market-specific language: reference target regions naturally

## What to Avoid
- Superlatives without proof ("best", "world-class", "leading")
- Consumer-facing language
- Passive voice
- Long sentences (max 20 words)

## Key Facts to Use
- Founded: 2011 · 15+ years in AC industry
- Factory: 30,000 sqm · China
- Capacity: 3 million units/year
- Lead time: 30 days standard · 10 days from stock
- Sampling: 7-day rapid sampling
- Defect rate: <0.1% · 100% leakage testing pre-shipment
- MOQ: Low MOQ
- Design: Italian design office
- Brand model: OBM (iCLIMA AIR brand, primary) · OEM (private label, secondary)
- Markets: Europe · North America · Africa · Latin America · Middle East
- Compressor brands: GMCC · Highly · Rechi · Sanyo
- Refrigerant: R32 (primary — do NOT mention R410a in copy)
- Certifications: CE · CB · UL · AHRI · RETIQ · RTCA · GMRK · ISO 9001
- Products: Inverter Split AC · On & Off Split AC · Solar AC · Free Match · Window AC · Duct/Cassette/Ceiling Floor AC
- Commercial range: up to 60K BTU
- Window AC range: 9K–24K BTU

## What NOT to Write
- Do not mention R410a — factory uses R32 as primary, R410a is being phased out globally
- Do not use specific factory address — use "China" only
- Do not use "B2B" in headings — use "distributors", "importers", "project buyers" instead
- Do not use OEM/ODM as the lead — OBM leads, OEM is secondary option
- Do not say "15-day lead time" — updated to 30 days (10 days from stock)
